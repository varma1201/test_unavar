  3. Fee for Services, Payment, Limitation of Liability

    The Audit fees is decided as {{total_cost}} for per {{no_of_outlets}} outlet. This fee is decided on the acceptance of proposal/work order issued by the client.   5. Impartiality

    Unavar Food Inspection and Certification Private Limited shall act impartially, and its policies and procedures for certification of persons shall be fair among all FBO's and shall not restrict certification on the grounds of undue financial or other limiting conditions. The Inspection Body shall be responsible for the impartiality of its certification and inspection activities and shall not allow commercial, financial or other pressures to compromise impartiality. The corporate policies and practices ensure that the highest standards of integrity are applied to all certification and inspection body activities. The management system has the necessary procedures, safeguards and tools to ensure that these policies are strictly upheld and enforced. The Audit service will not affect the trade associate with the clients and the quality of the audit will not be based on the trade associate with the clients.

  6. Confidentiality

    This Confidentiality Policy documents the confidentiality and non – disclosure duties and obligations of the employees and personnel of UNAVAR FOOD INSPECTION AND CERTIFICATION PRIVATE LIMITED. All information received by or available to UNAVAR FOOD INSPECTION AND CERTIFICATION PRIVATE LIMITED, staff, auditors, contractors or committee members (in whatever format) received in conducting Inspection activities, or during other certification activities, or during any dealings with an organization for any other reason shall be regarded as strictly confidential and shall not be divulged to any 3rd party without the express permission of the organization or individual concerned. All records will be retained in a secure manner, only accessible to authorized staff via either paper records or password controlled electronic records. Contractors will be limited to accessing information produced by them in conducting an audit. Records will only be made available to organizations who can demonstrate a legitimate (and legal) right to view those records and specifically to Accreditation Bodies. All staff, auditors, contractors, SME and other members will be required to agree to UNAVAR FOOD INSPECTION AND CERTIFICATION PRIVATE LIMITED, confidentiality policy and sign a confidentiality agreement. Contractors will also sign an agreement which also contains the responsibility to maintain confidentiality.

  1. Makes all necessary arrangements for the conducting the Inspection including providing documents to be audited, access to all processes and areas, records and personnel for the audit.
  2. Wherever necessary, permits the presence of QCI observers or trainee auditor/assessors or shadow auditors to be a part of the Inspection the accompanying auditor will be observing, witnessing and recording the information, interviews and Inspection will be carried forward by the lead auditor
  3. Wherever necessary, permits the presence of other observers or trainee auditor or shadow auditors to be a part of the Inspection from the organization. The accompanying auditor will be observing, witnessing and recording the information, interviews and Inspection will be carried forward by the lead auditor.
  4. Any misleading and unauthorized information given by FBO will lead to lead to Legal Implications.
  5. Inspection is executed as per the Inspection checklist and methodology of FSSAI Third Party Inspection regulations 2018.
  6. The FEs shall respond with corrective actions for the Major non-conformances and with evidence of closure to the inspection body within 24 hours of conducting the audits in case of major NCs and for minor NC within 15 Days Corrective Actions Plan to be submitted. These Corrective action or Closure evidence should be submitted by FE in format provided by the inspection body.
  7. Once Client NC report is shared by client and FE acknowledges the Inspection Report, the inspection body shall upload the audit report, with necessary evidences in FSSAI portal in case of FSSAI Third Party audit – Inspection services else the report is shared only to the Client.
  8. Auditors Safety should be ensured in all condition during course of audit by the FBO and there shouldn't be any situation which becomes a threat to the safety of the auditor
